Latent Textual Space: Pivot for Scaling Customized Text-to-Image and Video Generation

Jian Jin, Zhenbo Yu, Yang Shen, Zhenyong Fu, Jian Yang

Comparison with the Pre-trained Model

As stated in Section III.B of the manuscript, a key merit of LTS lies in its structural preservation capability and precise spatial–semantic correspondence. By leveraging these merits of LTS and the inherent denoising characteristics of diffusion models, LaTexBlend-v maintains the original motion and layout of the pre-trained model while seamlessly injecting the subject's identity. Therefore, LaTexBlend-v alleviates the motion and structure degradation exhibited by previous inflated customized T2V methods.
